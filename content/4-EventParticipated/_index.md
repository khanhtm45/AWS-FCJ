During my internship, I had the opportunity to participate in three technology events organized by AWS, focusing on Generative AI and modern software development. Each event was a memorable experience that provided new, interesting, and highly valuable knowledge about applying AI in practice, along with meaningful connections and wonderful moments.

### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)

&emsp;**Event Name:** AWS Cloud Day Vietnam 2025 – AI Edition

&emsp;**Theme:** _New Age Vietnam: From Vision to Value_

&emsp;**Date:** September 18, 2025

&emsp;**Location:** Vietnam

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee

&emsp;**Key Content:** The event focused on transforming AI vision into measurable value, covering topics on Generative AI, reference architectures (RAG, agentic workflows), data security, and deployment roadmap from PoC to Scale on AWS platform.

---

### [Event 2](4.2-Event2/)

&emsp;**Event Name:** AI-powered planning, design, and coding for modern software development

&emsp;**Format:** AWS Marketplace on-demand webinar

&emsp;**Date:** October 1, 2025

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee

&emsp;**Key Content:** Webinar demonstrating how to integrate Generative AI into the SDLC (planning, design, coding), including sprint planning, test generation, automated documentation/architecture generation, and applying zero-trust security for code analysis and architecture reviews.

---

### [Event 3](4.1-Event3/)

&emsp;**Event Name:** AI-Driven Development Lifecycle: Reshaping Software Engineering

&emsp;**Date & Time:** 14:00, October 3, 2025

&emsp;**Location:** AWS Event Hall, L26 Bitexco Tower, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee

&emsp;**Key Content:** Workshop introducing the AIDOC (AI Development Lifecycle) methodology, how to use Amazon Q Developer and Kiro to boost software development productivity, with emphasis on human-AI collaboration throughout the entire development lifecycle.
